Top three highlights of Hai Phong

hover for preview

Cat Ba Island

Only 30 kilometers from the city center, the island is a combination of sea, forest, and fascinating caves and grottoes. A prominent attraction you don't want to miss.

Pho Chieu Pagoda

Soak in the tranquil and serene beauty of this Buddhist pilgrimage. It stands tall overlooking a clear blue lake, with dragon-like structures guarding the sanctuary.

Flamboyant Flowers

The streets of coastal Hai Phong are adorned with scenic flamboyant flowers that bloom in late May, early June.
